[ENG] Comparisons between cutaneous and visceral Leishmaniasis with WHO data

 Nov 11, 2019
I recently found out that the World Health Organization (WHO) has a package available for R in CRAN. CRAN is an archive of packages, codes and documents in general for this programming language. Although the WHO package is not official, it contains very interesting data from this agency. Installation of necessary packages: library(WHO) library(dplyr) library(tidyverse) library(ggplot2) library(lubridate) codes <- get_codes(extra = TRUE) glimpse(codes) 3287 datasets on various topics are automatically downloaded with this package, some of them on leishmaniasis.
